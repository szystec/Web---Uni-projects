Write-Up
This is the very first website designed and created by me. For its creation I used XHTML 1.0 Strict and Cascade Style Sheet version 2.1, both of them fully compatible with the markup validation standards. I have not had any previous experience with XHTML and CSS, thus this assignment was of a great significance for me.
At the beginning I did not even know how to start the proper XHTML file.
Fortunately, Stanislaw Klimaszewski helped me a lot with understanding the basis of designing new website and also guided me to a very useful site: w3schools.com where not only all helpful parameters, but also the examples of how to use them were presented.
For banner in the header section I used my own photo taken in Aberystwyth, background was found on freevectorgallery.com.
On my site one can easily see two ECMA scripts: the first one desired to show the last date of website modification, placed in the page footer section, and the second one in home directory.
I have also changed links appearance to be more user-friendly.
